Our Story

🏫 Story

District Spanish was established in 2016 by Gerardo Domínguez as a community-focused initiative in the Washington D.C. metropolitan area. We became an online school in 2020, but our commitment to community-focused learning hasn't changed — we still mix online classes with in-person events and fiestas in the DMV area. With roots firmly planted in community spirit and cultural pride, District Spanish remains dedicated to fostering connections and celebrating the joy of language learning.

🎯 Mission

District Spanish is a Spanish education center that encourages and motivates students to proactively reach their language learning objectives. We promote student-teacher relationships, customize learning curriculum, and provide great spaces and services to reach customer satisfaction. Our passionate team of language coaches guides students to reach their full potential, immersing them in the culture of Spanish-speaking countries — with the ultimate goal of having a fun time learning.

🌟 Vision

District Spanish wants to foster Spanish immersion inside and outside the classroom by building a strong Spanish-speaking community, compiling a wealth of language resources, and offering an array of cultural immersion activities to encourage learning.
