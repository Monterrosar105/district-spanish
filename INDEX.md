## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Want to change colors?
Find `--primary-color` in `css/style.css` and change the hex code.
All buttons, accents, and highlights update automatically.

### Want to add your logo?
Replace `images/logo.svg` and `images/logo-tab.svg` with your files.
Support for PNG, SVG, or JPG.

### Want to add teacher photos?
Replace `images/teachers/teacher*.svg` with your JPG/PNG files.
Recommended: 300x300px, < 100KB each.

### Want to change email/WhatsApp?
Search `index.html` for `team@districtspanish.com` and `14155552671`.
Update to your information.

### Want to update pricing?
Find pricing section in `index.html` around line 470.
Edit prices, features, and descriptions.

### Want to change teacher information?
Find teacher section around line 260.
Update names, countries, specialties, and taglines.

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ **Always test mobile view** (F12 → Device Toolbar)

✅ **Compress images before uploading** (TinyPNG.com)

✅ **Enable GZIP on server** for faster loading

✅ **Use Cloudflare (free)** for global CDN

✅ **Setup Google Analytics** to track visitors

✅ **Keep backups** of customized files

✅ **Clear cache** (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) if changes don't show

✅ **Test WhatsApp link** before launching

✅ **Verify contact form** works in your email

✅ **Monitor uptime** after launch
